Save, share & discover the best moments in video

== Description ==

Use theMoment to control how Google's [new video search](https://www.blog.google/products/search/key-moments-video-search/ "Search helps you find key moments in videos") lists your videos.

Google now lists videos with "key moments" first in line. Put your video's best moments forward to attract more visitors.

*   As a publisher, tap one simple button in the corner of your videos to pick key moments.
*   Videos with key moments now appear first in Google's new video search results.
*   Display these same key moments on your site's videos and let visitors jump to specific scenes.
*   Let visitors pick and share the moments they want to shout about.
*   Track the exact moments that attract the most visitors.

[More >](https://streameditor.tv/use_case_vseo_wordpress.php/ "Introducing vSEO")

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/themoment` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Use the Settings->themoment screen to configure the plugin
1. On Settings->themoment screen, use the Login button on the top-right to log into theMoment.

[More >](https://streameditor.tv/how_to_wordpress.php/ "How to Install theMoment")

= Does theMoment violate copyright law? =

No. theMoment simply allows Publishers, Creators and viewers to jump to specific points in original videos. theMoment does not copy, move, or modify the original video.
